My Roots

I was born in a city called Oxnard, California. It is a small simple town known for its strwaberry fields.

I lived there until around my fourth birthday when we moved to Westland, Michigan.

We lived in Westland for a few years before moving in Canton, Michigan where I lived for the next twelve years.

After living in Canton for my childhood we eventually moved to where I am now, Ypsilanti, Michigan.

Ypsilanti may not be the safest city in Michigan but the downtown area has some great history to it.

Ypsilanti also has the university that I attend, Eastern Michigan University, which I have attended for the past three years.
